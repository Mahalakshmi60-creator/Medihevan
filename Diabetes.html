DIABETES

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic health condition that affects how the body converts food into energy. It is characterized by elevated levels of blood glucose (sugar) due to insufficient production or improper use of insulin, a hormone produced by the pancreas. If left unmanaged, diabetes can lead to severe complications.

Types of Diabetes?

1.Type1 Diabetes
2.Type2 Diabetes
3.Gestational Diabetes
4.Prediabetes

Symptoms:

1.Increased thirst and hunger
2.Frequent urination
3.Unexplained weight loss
4.Fatigue
5.Blurred vision
6.Slow-healing wounds
7.Frequent infections

Diagnosis:

1.Blood tests
2.Criteria

Prevention:

1.Maintain a healthy weight
2.Adopt a balanced diet rich in fiber and less in sugar
3.Exercise regularly
4.Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol intake
5.Regular check-ups if at risk

Early detection and management can help prevent complications and improve the quality of life for those with diabetes.
