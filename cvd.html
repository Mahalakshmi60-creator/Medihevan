CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASES

What is Cardiovascular Diseases?

Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) are a group of disorders affecting the heart and blood vessels. They are among the leading causes of death worldwide, often linked to lifestyle, genetic factors, and environmental influences.

Types of Cardiovascular Diseases?

1.Coronary Artery Disease(CAD)
2.Hypertension
3.Stroke
4.Heart Failure
5.Arrhythmias
6.Peripheral Artery Disease
7.Congenital Heart disease
8.Rheumatic Heart disease

Symptoms f CVDs:

1.Chest pain or discomfort
2.Shortness of breath
3.Fatigue
4.Dizziness or fainting
5.Swelling in the legs, ankles, or feet
6.Rapid or irregular heartbeat

Diagnosis:

1.Blood tests
2.ECG
3.Echocardiogram
4.Stress tests
5.Angiography
6.CT or MRI scans

Treatment:

1.Lifestyle changes:
Diet, exercise, and weight management.
2.Medications:
Antihypertensives, statins, anticoagulants, or antiplatelets
3.Surgical Interventions:
Valve repair or replacement



CVDs are preventable and manageable with early detection, a healthy lifestyle, and appropriate medical care.
